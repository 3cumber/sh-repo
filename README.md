# 🧭 Shawarma Compass

A mobile web app that points you toward the nearest shawarma joint and keeps the name hidden until you're close enough to smell it.

**[Open the app](https://3cumber.github.io/sh-repo/)**

---

## What it does

- Compass needle points at the closest shawarma spot
- Name stays masked until you're within your reveal distance
- Logs visits, lets you rate and review each spot
- Awards and rank progression as you explore
- Share your progress as a card

## How to use

Open the link on your phone over HTTPS, tap to allow location and compass access, and start walking.

Works best on iPhone. Android compass heading is best-effort.

## Data

Spots are pulled live from [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org). If something is missing near you, use the **Log a spot** button inside the app.
